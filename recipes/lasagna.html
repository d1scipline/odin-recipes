Lasagna

Description

To make a simple lasagna, start by preheating your oven to 375째F (190째C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a large skillet, brown 1 lb of ground beef (or turkey) with 1 diced onion and 2 minced garlic cloves. Once browned, stir in 2 cups of marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and a pinch of dried oregano. In a bowl, combine 15 oz of ricotta cheese with 1 beaten egg, 2 cups of shredded mozzarella, and a pinch of salt. To assemble, spread a layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, then the ricotta mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (or turkey)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef (or turkey) over medium heat with the diced onion and minced garlic until the meat is fully cooked and the onions are soft.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the meat mixture. Stir in salt, pepper, and dried oregano. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, the beaten egg, shredded mozzarella, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish.
  7. Top with extra shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
